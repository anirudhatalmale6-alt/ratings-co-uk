NIS2 Compliance Statement

Last updated: 20 May 2026

1. Scope

This statement outlines Ratings.co.uk's compliance with the Network and Information Security Directive (EU) 2022/2555 (NIS2), which establishes cybersecurity risk management and reporting obligations for essential and important entities operating within the European Union.

2. Risk Management Measures

Ratings.co.uk implements appropriate and proportionate technical, operational, and organisational measures to manage risks posed to the security of network and information systems, including: policies on risk analysis and information system security; incident handling procedures; business continuity and crisis management; supply chain security; and security in network and information systems acquisition, development, and maintenance.

3. Incident Reporting

In accordance with NIS2 Article 23, Ratings.co.uk maintains procedures for reporting significant incidents to the relevant competent authority. Early warnings are issued within 24 hours of becoming aware of a significant incident, with full incident notifications within 72 hours.

4. Supply Chain Security

We assess and manage cybersecurity risks within our supply chain, ensuring that service providers and partners maintain adequate security standards consistent with NIS2 requirements.

5. Governance

Management is responsible for overseeing and approving cybersecurity risk management measures. Staff members receive regular cybersecurity awareness training to identify and respond to threats effectively.

6. Technical Measures

Our infrastructure employs: encryption of data in transit and at rest; access control and authentication mechanisms; continuous monitoring and logging; vulnerability management and regular security assessments; secure development lifecycle practices.
